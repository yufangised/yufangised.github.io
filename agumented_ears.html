agumented ears / 2012 / ciid

#design #electronics #art installation #processing #arduino

Augmented Ears is a human enhancement concept that explores the idea of additional and augmented hearing senses in a far-flung future scenario.

The overwhelming information in the modern cities that people experience every day has been seen as the backdrop in our exploration of future human senses and perception. Through Augmented ears, people can experience the world around in a more emotional way - blocking the eyesight while providing eight ears, which can filter and hear different frequencies of sound. It now becomes possible to filter all unwanted noise away and compose a personal sound landscape by picking one frequency or mixing two different frequencies.

It is a working prototype built by hacking together an old horse-riding helmet with a set of cheap headphones. Each ear with a magnet in the center can be placed on the "main ears" on the helmet and it works as a switch, which is mapped with a certain frequency of sound.

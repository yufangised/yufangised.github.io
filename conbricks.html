YUFAN WANG

CONBRICKS / 2017 / LEGO Private


This project started as a fun experiment in blending two contrasting materials: LEGO and concrete. I used LEGO bricks to build molds, poured in concrete, and intentionally designed some LEGO elements to remain a part of the final object. What came out of it is a series of unique, multifunctional desktop pieces that bring together the raw, industrial feel of concrete with the colorful, modular charm of LEGO.

Each piece is designed to be more than just a beautiful object. It serves a purposeâ€”you could use it to hold pens, jewelry, or other small items, making it a practical addition to any desktop. The buildable LEGO interface adds an extra layer of playfulness, allowing you to stack more LEGO bricks and customize the piece however you want.

Fireworks Lantern is a creative lantern designed using PCB (Printed Circuit Board) as the main material. It can interact with ambient sound (especially in the context of the fireworks during the Chinese new year) by lighting LED lights reacting to the volume of sound at a sensitive frequency range.

This project is my first attempt to explore the creative usages of PCB to create objects, forms not only with the functional value but also the aesthetic and culture values. It brings together two Chinese traditional elements -- the lantern and the fireworks, which give a the strong and interesting visual contrast between the tradition and the sense of technology.

INSTAGRAM LIKE BOT / 2025



This project starts as a simple conversation with a gallery owner who suggested that I should engage more on Instagram by liking other artists’ posts to increase visibility for my own profile. Intrigued, I followed his advice and tried it for two weeks—browsing and liking art posts manually. To my surprise, it led to a noticeable boost in engagement and new followers.

Realizing the potential of this strategy, I wondered: why not automate the process? My initial idea was to build a robotic arm that would physically tap the like button whenever it detected an art-related post. However, as I explored further, I discovered a simplier and more elegant solution. Instead of using hardware, I leveraged iPhone’s built-in Voice Control to trigger actions using voice commands. By mirroring my phone’s screen to my computer, I was able to process what “I” saw and automate interactions based on content recognition.

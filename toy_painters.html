YUFAN WANG

TOY PAINTERS / 2024 / Just For Fun



I work at LEGO.

A toy company.

In our office,

there is a big box full of competitor toys.

We called it the "toy graveyard".

I passed by and heard the toys screaming:

"Do not abandon us!"

I agreed,

fun and talented toys shouldn’t be overlooked.

So I offered the best ones a new job:

toy painters.




Masterpiece


Let’s take a moment to appreciate the masterpiece created by the talented toy artists!
